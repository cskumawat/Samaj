# Code of Conduct

Samaj contributors must behave professionally and prioritize lawful, defensive use.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful.
- Be factual.
- Provide evidence for technical claims.
- Respect legal boundaries.
- Handle security discussions responsibly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Encouraging unauthorized access.
- Sharing credential theft workflows.
- Submitting malware, phishing, botnet, persistence, or evasion features.
- Harassment or discriminatory conduct.
- Misrepresenting unverified results as facts.

Reports can be handled by the maintainers of the repository.
